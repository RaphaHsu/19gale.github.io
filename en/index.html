"HEARThermo" – Protect the Health of the First-Line Medical Staff

Surveillance and prediction are two important parts in avoiding the spread of diseases. The NCKU development team collaborates with ARBOR Technology Corp. to develop the "HEARThermo" which can continuously monitor the changes in body temperature and the heart rate and rhythm.

HEARThermo can be used as an indicator to predict infections and the symptoms of infectious diseases. Through algorithm, it can monitor the user’s conditions anytime and let the user be alert to sudden changes in personal symptoms. There are more than 150 medical staff fighting against COVID-19 who are wearing the "HEARThermo" in NCKU Hospital. One of the medical staff said, "When I take care of patients, I worry about my health condition. However, there is a device that can monitor my body temperature, I feel safe at work."

Application Scenario

The General Public Healthcare

The abnormal changes in body temperature are the obvious symptoms of all infectious diseases. Current situations such as COVID-19 bring about a risk of infections to the worldwide. Monitoring body temperature becomes essential in finding diseases as minor variations may indicate a potential disease threat.

Care AI Heartwarming Solution can simultaneously track a large number of suspected cases, automatically report an individual’s health status, and can detect the abnormal symptoms in advance to reduce the risk of virus spread.

Employee Healthcare

Employee Health is an important part of Business Continuity Planning (BCP).

In Care AI Heartwarming Solution, it is necessary to keep abreast of the employee’s health status to give early warnings. In addition, an abnormal condition will be automatically reported in order to avoid health risks in the workplace and reduce the business losses.

The Disabled and the Elderly Healthcare

It is common for the disabled or the elderly to have mobility limitations, activity limitations or multimorbidity. The decline of physiological functions or the influence of internal and external factors for them may result in unpredictable accidents. Therefore, it is extremely important to observe the changes of their physical conditions.

Care AI Heartwarming Solution can substitute for caregivers to continuously track the health conditions of the patients. The abnormal conditions will be reported instantly so that the caregivers or the medical staff can provide assistance in the golden hour.
